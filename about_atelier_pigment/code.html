A Legacy of Color

Preserving the alchemy of traditional pigment making for the modern architectural canvas.

Our Vision

To redefine the relationship between light and living spaces through the lens of pure, mineral pigments. We see walls not as boundaries, but as vibrant tapestries that breathe and evolve with the passing day.

The Mission

Atelier Pigments is dedicated to the meticulous curation and hand-blending of artisanal paints. We bridge the gap between historic conservation and contemporary design, ensuring every brushstroke carries the weight of authenticity and the brilliance of nature.

The Timeline

Forty Years of Artistry

1984

The First Mill

Founded in a small workshop in Lyon, our journey began with a single manual stone mill and a passion for mineral ochres.

2002

The Lapis Collection

Launched our signature Ultramarine Lapis line, reviving 14th-century techniques for modern luxury interiors.

2024

The Digital Atelier

Introducing carbon-neutral logistics and our custom color-matching AI, blending heritage with the future.
